Cory Benfield

is an open source Python developer working full-time for HPE in the Python HTTP community. He's a Requests core contributor, a urllib3 core contributor, and the lead maintainer on the Hyper project, a toolbox of projects for working with HTTP and HTTP/2. He loves computer networks, film and TV, and travel.

Baptiste Mispelon

Web developer from France based in Budapest, Hungary.
Lover of all things Python and Django.
Has a bad habbit of turning everything he does into a conference.
Firm believer in global emojification.

Daniele Procida

Daniele was the organiser of Django Weekend Cardiff, and a member of the organising committee of DjangoCon Europe 2014. He’s also leading the organisation of Python Namibia 2015, the first-ever Python conference to be held in the country. He is a member of the Django Project core team, and works for Divio. At Divio he works on django CMS, Aldryn and other fun Django-based projects.
