PyCon is coming to Slovakia!

PyCon SK is a community-organized conference for the Python programming language. The inaugural PyCon SK will be held in Bratislava on 11th - 13th March 2016.

We are happy to announce that apart from two days of interesting talks, we will organize workshops that will be suitable for absolute beginners.

CC by Taavi Burns

Tickets We have launched the ticket presale, you can buy early bird tickets for a special price.

If you want support us, become a volunteer, or help with organizing of PyCon SK (which is run entirely by volunteers) in any other way, we will be happy if you let us know.

In the meantime you can visit our regular meetups.
