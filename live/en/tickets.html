Tickets

Registration is now open. You have the opportunity to purchase discounted tickets!

By purchasing a ticket you agree to abide by the Code of Conduct.

Which ticket should I buy?

Supporter: This ticket is designed for participants who want to make their name (company) be visible in the list of supporters on our website.
Standard: This ticket is designed for all conference participants.
Student: This ticket is designed for students. Don't forget to bring your valid student ID (ISIC) to the registration.

Is there any another possibility to get to PyCon?

We have a financial aid program allowing some visitors to come to PyCon for free. Tell us why we should give you the ticket.
If you are less than 18 years old and come accompanied by a parent, you can just purchase one ticket only for the parent, but you have to inform us in advance to issue an another ticket for the child.
The financial aid program is designed for socially disadvantaged individuals that would otherwise not be able to attend the conference. Don't hesitate to contact us if you want to apply for it.

What is included in the ticket price?

  • two days of awesome talks
  • delicious lunch on both days
  • coffee and cake during breaks
  • t-shirt and a bag full of surprises
  • invitation for Friday's informal party

Do you offer group discounts?

If you would like to buy more than 10 tickets please contact us by email at info@pycon.sk.

I don't use PayPal, is there any other way to buy a ticket?

We also accept wire transfer, or Bitcoin. Please send us a list of tickets you are interested in and after we receive the payment, we will issue and send the requested tickets to you by email.

Where does the money from tickets go?

PyCon is completely organized by volunteers. We do believe that such conferences should be organized to promote programming and Open Source. All money collected from the ticket sales will be used to cover the expenses related to organizing the conference. The SPy civic association, as the organizer, commits to transparently disclose all the costs associated with organizing PyCon at the end of the conference. The civic association was created to support Python and programming in general, you can read more about our vision and objectives.
